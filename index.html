    • Sticky Wings

    Ingredients

    • frying oil
    • 4 chicken breasts
    • 2 cups flour
    • 4 tablespoons salt
    • 4 teaspoons pepper
    • 4 teaspoons cayenne pepper
    • 2 teaspoons paprika
    • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 + 1/2 cups milk
    • 3/4 cup buffalo sauce
    • 3/4 cup brown sugar

    Instructions

    1. Cut the chicken into thin strips.
    2. Mix the eggs and milk together in a bowl.
    3. Mix the dry flour and spices in a separate bowl.
    4. Dip the chicken in the egg mixture then coat well with the flour mixture.
    5. Heat the oil to 350째F and fry chicken in batches. Cook the chicken until it is golden brown and the internal temperature is at least 165째F.
    6. Heat buffalo sauce and brown sugar over medium heat while stirring until brown sugar is dissolved.

    Fluffy Breadsticks

    Ingredients

    • 1 + 1/2 cup warm water
    • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 4 cups flour
    • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons melted butter

    Instructions

    1. Dissolve the sugar in the warm water (105-115째F), then dissolve the yeast.
    2. Add the remaining dry ingredients, yeast mixture, and olive oil to a large stand mixer bowl. Knead the dough for eight minutes.
    3. Coat a 13"x9" pan with oil. Stretch the dough into the pan and cover with a wet cloth. Let the dough rise for one hour.
    4. Preheat the oven to 425째F.
    5. Spread the butter over the top of the dough.
    6. Cut the dough into 3/4" thick slices. This is easiest to do with a large spatula.
    7. Cook breadsticks in the oven for 20 minutes. You may need to cover the pan with tin foil to prevent the top from burning
    8. Optionally serve the breadsticks with marinara sauce or ranch.

    Easy Truffles

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
    • 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 cup dark milk chocolate chips

    Instructions

    1. First, we need to make the ganache. Heat the heavy cream over low heat in until it starts to simmer, then mix in the milk chocolate chips. Stir until well combined.
    2. Place the ganache in the freezer until solid but not frozen (about 15 minutes).
    3. Take the ganache out of the freezer and roll into 1/4-inch balls. Place the rolled ganache back in the freeze for 5 more minutes.
    4. Melt the dark chocolate. Dip each ganache ball into the melted chocolate and set aside to solidify.

    Sweet Rice

    Ingredients

    • cooked white rice
    • 1 can coconut milk
    • 1 + 1/4 cup sugar
    • sliced mango (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Cook your rice! Just about any type of white rice makes great sweet rice!
    2. Place coconut oil and sugar in a pan and heat over medium heat until it start to simmer.
    3. Mix the sauce with rice. This sauce is flavorful and strong, so only add enough to lightly coat the rice.
    4. Optionally serve with sliced mangos on top.
